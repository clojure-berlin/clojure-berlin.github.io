# Clojure+Elixir pre BOBKONF drinks

Let's grab some drinks together to warmup for [BOB Konf](https://bobkonf.de/) starting the next day.

This is a joint meetup with the Elixir Berlin community to celebrate functional programming. Unconference format — grab a table and discuss topics of interest in small groups.

The venue is near Rosa-Luxemburg-Platz, a brief U2 ride from BOB Konf at Potsdamer Platz.

Date: 12th March
Location: [Volksbar](https://maps.apple.com/?q=Volksbar&address=Rosa-Luxemburg-Stra%C3%9Fe+39,+10178+Berlin)

Please RSVP on Luma, and also on the [Elixir Berlin Meetup page](https://www.meetup.com/elixir-berlin/events/313600065/) for capacity planning.
